Q1 Plan

Day 1 to Day 90.

Three phases. Five actions per phase. Each one ships a measurable thing — not a strategy slide.

Publish Blog Post 1 (coworking cost breakdown)

Metric:indexed in Google within 7 days; 500+ organic visits and top-20 rank for "coworking space" within 30 days.
Dependency:none (drafted, ready to ship).
Why now:zero-budget proof that non-branded content moves the needle on a 4,400/mo keyword WeWork currently doesn't rank for.

Publish Blog Post 2 (Bengaluru neighbourhood guide)

Metric:300+ organic visits in 30 days; 2+ internal links from existing site pages live.
Dependency:none (drafted, cross-linked to Post 1).
Why now:stacks intent against Post 1, builds the topical cluster the audit recommends, and seeds the Bengaluru city page work in Phase 2.

Launch the 5-post LinkedIn series

Metric:1 post per week for 5 weeks; 50K+ combined impressions; 3+ enterprise inbound conversations attributed to the series.
Dependency:30-min review with the CMO on the two executive-voice posts.
Why now:the executive voice posts cost nothing, signal an ability to write in leadership tone from day one, and seed the Tier-2 narrative externally before the internal pilot ask.

Run the non-branded keyword gap audit live with the SEO/content team

Metric:prioritised backlog of 40 target keywords (city, building, product) shared with content + dev leads by Day 21; alignment meeting closed with named owners per cluster.
Dependency:1 hour each from SEO lead and web dev lead.
Why now:the audit shows cofynd.com pulls 3× WeWork's traffic from the same keyword universe at lower DR — this is the single highest-leverage finding and the team needs to see the numbers, not the slides.

Stand up weekly non-branded organic dashboard

Metric:live Tableau/Looker view tracking non-branded sessions, top-50 keyword count, and conversion to demo request — shared with the CMO by Day 30.
Dependency:GA4 + Search Console access.
Why now:every action that follows needs a measurement spine; without it Q1 wins are anecdotes.

Ship city landing pages for Bengaluru, Noida, Mumbai

Metric:all 3 pages live by Day 55; combined 1,500+ organic visits/month by Day 90; top-10 rank for at least one city's "coworking space in [city]" term.
Dependency:web dev (est. 8 dev days), 1 designer week.
Why now:4,100–5,600/mo opportunity confirmed in the audit; Awfis already ranks here and WeWork is absent. This is the largest ROI block in the backlog.

Rebuild the Virtual Office product page

Metric:live by Day 60; ranks top-20 for "virtual office" within 60 days of publish; 20+ qualified VO leads in first month post-launch.
Dependency:product marketing input on VO pricing tiers, web dev (3 days).
Why now:3,000/mo standalone opportunity, Awfis already wins this term, and VO is a low-touch revenue line that maps cleanly to non-branded acquisition.

Launch a paid LinkedIn acquisition test against Tier-1 enterprise decision-makers

Metric:₹2 lakh test budget; CPL under ₹3,500; 8+ booked enterprise demos in 30 days.
Dependency:₹2L paid budget approval, HubSpot landing page setup.
Why now:tests whether the LinkedIn organic traction from Days 1–30 converts under paid amplification, and gives a CAC benchmark before the Tier-2 pilot needs one.

Internal linking overhaul across top 50 pages

Metric:200+ contextual internal links added; homepage traffic concentration drops from 100% to under 80%; 15+ secondary pages enter top-100 rankings.
Dependency:SEO contractor or 1 week of focused time.
Why now:the audit's #7 priority but nearly free; compounds every other content asset shipped in Phase 1 and Phase 2.

Publish 2 more blog posts in the cost/comparison cluster

Metric:live by Day 60; combined 800+ organic visits in first 30 days post-publish.
Dependency:content writer or 1 week of focused time.
Why now:Posts 1 and 2 will have proven the cluster works; doubling down is cheaper than starting a new content vertical.

Kick off the Kochi pilot 90-day demand validation gate

Metric:200+ qualified Infopark/Smartworld outreach contacts; 20+ priced LOI conversations; clear go/no-go signal (≥10% conversion on qualified outreach) by Day 90.
Dependency:real estate + sales coordination, ₹3L outbound + landing page budget.
Why now:the Deal Strategist's kill switch fires before fit-out spend; if signal is weak, the ₹16 cr redirects to Bengaluru centre #2 with no capex burned.

Ship building/neighbourhood landing pages for top 10 tech parks

Metric:10 pages live by Day 90; ranking top-30 for at least 4 tech park keywords (8,100/mo opportunity from 3 keywords alone).
Dependency:web dev (12 dev days), local content research.
Why now:Awfis is already capturing prestige skytech, dsl abacus, vios tower — every month WeWork delays, Awfis builds more authority on terms WeWork's centres physically sit inside.

Launch Kochi pre-launch landing page + waitlist

Metric:300+ waitlist signups from Infopark-targeted outreach + paid; signups feed directly into the LOI pipeline above.
Dependency:web dev (2 days), ₹1L paid budget.
Why now:gives the Tier-2 demand validation a measurable digital surface and produces the priced LOI evidence the gating condition requires.

Hand off content engine to a hired/contracted writer

Metric:1 new ranking-ready post published per week from Day 75 onward; in-house content time drops below 20%.
Dependency:1 hire or contractor (₹60K–₹80K/mo).
Why now:the engine works in Phase 2; scaling it requires the operator off the keyboard and onto strategy by Day 90.

Expand city pages to Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, Gurgaon

Metric:all 4 live by Day 90; combined 800+ organic visits in first month.
Dependency:web dev (8 dev days).
Why now:template exists from Phase 2, marginal cost per page is low, and these complete the Tier-1 city footprint before Q2 attention shifts to Tier-2.
By Day 90: non-branded organic traffic up from baseline ~9K/mo to a measurable 18K–22K/mo on a clear upward curve. Two blog posts ranking page 1 or 2. Seven city pages, ten building pages, and a rebuilt Virtual Office page live. 8+ enterprise demos sourced from LinkedIn paid. A Kochi pilot with either a green light backed by 50+ priced LOIs, or a clean kill and ₹16 cr redirected. One dashboard that tells the CMO every Monday whether the engine is accelerating or stalling.

Campaign Concept

If WeWork launches Kochi — this is what runs.

One product, one city, one claim: the building has finally caught up to the work.

The Building Catches Up.

Kochi's work grew up. Its workplace just did too.

WeWork opens in Kochi — the first enterprise-grade flex built for the GCCs and founders already here.

Audience
GCC India heads and global operations leaders evaluating Kochi for a captive build-out.
Headline
Your London board. Your Kochi floor. Same standard.
Body
WeWork opens in Kochi with the same enterprise spec your teams use in Bengaluru, Mumbai and Manila. Signed by global firms across 50+ India centres. Now ready for the 120 GCCs Infopark Phase 3 is built to hold.
Proof point
120 GCCs targeted at Infopark by 2031.
Lead format
LinkedIn document post + 1:1 BD outreach sequence to named GCC leaders.
Audience
Mid-market Indian CEOs and CFOs (100–500 employees) sizing up a Kochi expansion or HQ shift.
Headline
Move to Kochi in 30 days. Not 9 months.
Body
A WeWork Private Office in Kochi runs ₹15,000 a seat — fully fitted, fully serviced, fully off your balance sheet. No fit-out capex, no lease lock-in, no broker. Scale up or down by the floor.
Proof point
₹15,000 Private Office seat / ₹13,200 blended — and zero capex.
Lead format
60-second founder film + a gated TCO calculator landing page.
Audience
Series B+ founders running split teams between Bengaluru/Mumbai and Kochi.
Headline
Hire in Kochi this quarter. Decide the lease never.
Body
All Access from ₹9,000 a seat across every WeWork in India. Land your Kochi engineering pod on Monday, plug into Bengaluru on Thursday, walk away in twelve months if the plan changes. The network is the perk.
Proof point
₹9,000 All Access — 21% below the Tier-1 equivalent.
Lead format
WhatsApp creative + founder-network landing page, seeded through Kerala Startup Mission and YC/Sequoia India alumni groups.

Channel mix

LinkedIn (organic + paid) 35% Highest-intent reach for GCC leaders, CFOs and Series B+ founders; carries the document post, founder film and ABM retargeting.
OOH (Kochi-specific) 20% Geo-fenced to Infopark Phase 1 entry, Kakkanad corridor and airport arrivals — the three roads every target audience drives daily.
Performance / Search 15% Captures intent on "office space Kochi," "Infopark coworking," "GCC setup Kochi" — narrow, conversion-led, not a brand spend.
PR / Earned media 15% Anchors the "first premium flex in Kochi" narrative in ET, Mint, YourStory and BusinessLine ahead of the centre opening.
Direct outreach / Events 15% A founder dinner at the centre, an Infopark CEO breakfast, and a 200-name BD list — this is where signed LOIs come from.
